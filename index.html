Our Products

Track-Bill

Visit Website

Are you tired with signing the invoice manually?

Would you like to access your invoices from anywhere and approve the invoice with one click?

Do you want to find an invoice document within a second with a fingerprint?

Do you want full transparency and control over the payment?

Try our Track-Bill, a cloud-based online solution for managing your approval invoices and payments. It allows you to create your own approval matrix and claim invoices online.

Key Features:

  • Cloud storage for receipts
  • Invoice approval notifications
  • Custom approval matrix
  • Online invoice claiming
  • Approval functions

Maintenance Pro

A web-based platform for managing your device maintenance intervals. This equipment management software maintains your equipment database and helps streamline maintenance operations.

Key Features:

  • Maintenance schedule tracking
  • Complete equipment maintenance history
  • Breakdown records
  • Spare parts change history
  • Fuel consumption monitoring
  • Equipment cost history
